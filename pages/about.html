Our Story

About LEARN

We built LEARN to make quality English education accessible to everyone, everywhere.

What is LEARN?

LEARN is a two-sided marketplace that connects English learners with qualified, verified C1-level tutors for one-on-one online lessons. Whether you're a student preparing for an exam, a professional improving your business English, or a parent looking for lessons for your child — LEARN has a tutor for you.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to a great English tutor. Our mission is simple: remove the barriers between motivated learners and expert teachers. No subscriptions, no complicated setup — just book a lesson and start learning.

Quality First

Every tutor is manually reviewed and must hold a C1 English certification before joining the platform.

Flexible Learning

Book lessons that fit your schedule. Learn from anywhere in the world, at any time that works for you.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. Tutors set their own rates and you only pay when you book. Simple and fair.

Who is it for?

LEARN serves all types of English learners — kids, teenagers, adults, and professionals. Our tutors specialise in conversation, business English, IELTS and TOEFL preparation, grammar, pronunciation, and more.
