LEARN – Business Model &
Revenue Plan
1. Business Model
Type: Two-Sided Marketplace Platform
The platform connects C1 English students with qualified tutors.
It manages:
• Tutor applications and approvals
• Student bookings
• Video lesson access
• Payment processing
• Activity monitoring
2. Revenue Model
Commission-Based Only
• Students pay automatically when booking a lesson.
• The platform deducts a commission (e.g., 20%) from each payment.
• The remaining amount is transferred to the tutor’s account.
• No subscription plans for tutors or students.
• All payments are processed via integrated gateways (e.g., PayPal).
3. Monetization & Payment Plan
1. Lesson Booking Commissions
• Each booking triggers automatic payment collection.
• Commission (e.g., 20%) is deducted instantly.
• Tutor earnings are tracked in their account for payout.
2. Tutor Payouts
• Tutors are paid monthly, not immediately.
• Admin has a manual payout option for flexibility.
• Earnings, commissions, and payouts are recorded for transparency.
3. No Subscription Fees
• Neither tutors nor students pay monthly fees.
• Revenue is solely commission-based.
4. Revenue Source
• Income comes exclusively from lessons booked online.
• Each lesson payment includes a commission deduction.
• Tutors’ earnings are tracked for monthly payouts.
• No ads, subscriptions, or alternative monetization streams.
Summary:
The system is simple and focused — revenue is directly tied to lessons sold online.
5. Target Users
• C1 English level students only
• All age groups: kids, teens, adults
6. Core Platform Requirements
• Student sign-up / login
• Tutor application & approval system
• Lesson booking & scheduling
• Video lesson access
• Integrated payment gateway (e.g., PayPal)
• Earnings & commission tracking
• Admin dashboard for monitoring and payouts
